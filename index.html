About Our Company

Why Should You Choose Us?

Sawasdee Heights is a premium residential project strategically located near Punjabi Bagh and Pitampura, in the heart of Delhi. With seamless connectivity to major landmarks such as the Ring Road, Shakurpur Metro Station, and Netaji Subhash Place (NSP), this project ensures convenience for both families and professionals.

The development comprises four iconic towers—Sapphire, Ruby, Emerald, and Diamond—offering spacious 3BHK apartments with premium interiors such as marble flooring, modular kitchens, and wooden floors in master bedrooms. Constructed using advanced Mivan Shuttering Technology, it promises durability, superior finishing, and sustainability.

Sawasdee Heights is strategically located near Punjabi Bagh and Pitampura in the heart of Delhi. It is within walking distance of the Ring Road and Shakurpur Metro Station, and just 1 km from Netaji Subhash Place (NSP), a major commercial hub. The project also offers easy access to Britannia Road, Lawrence Road, and other key areas.

The project features a range of world-class amenities, including swimming pools, a gymnasium, a kids’ play area, a luxurious clubhouse, and eco-friendly systems like rainwater harvesting and energy-efficient technologies. Vehicle-free podium areas provide safe and peaceful spaces for relaxation and leisure.

Sawasdee Heights offers premium 3BHK apartments across four iconic towers: Sapphire, Ruby, Emerald, and Diamond. The apartments range in size from 2271 sq. ft. to 2460 sq. ft., with configurations that include smart layouts, spacious balconies, centralized lobbies, and premium interiors like marble flooring, modular kitchens, and wooden floors in master bedrooms.

Sawasdee Heights is a RERA-registered project (DLRERA2023P0012) with an expected possession date of December 2025. Pricing starts at ₹19,000 per sq. ft., with flexible payment plans such as the 35-35-30 and 50-50 options. Additional charges include ₹5,00,000 per covered parking slot and optional power backup at ₹25,000 per KVA.
